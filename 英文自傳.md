### 【University Capstone Project】
During my capstone project, I developed an educational learning platform integrated with generative AI. The platform featured an AI learning companion that transformed students into “mentors,” triggering the “Learning by Teaching” mechanism to deepen knowledge internalization.
*   **Performance Optimization Decisions**: Due to limitations in on-premise GPU inference speed, I switched to **Groq’s** high-speed inference service to connect with LLMs, achieving a smooth, near “zero-latency” conversational experience.
*   **On-Premise Computing Management**: I retained **GPUstack** on local servers to deploy embedding models for content vectorization and RAG retrieval. This approach ensured retrieval accuracy while effectively offloading and reducing cloud computing costs.
*   **Impact**: By successfully integrating “on-premise vector retrieval” with “high-speed cloud generation,” I overcame performance bottlenecks under constrained resources and built a real-time, text-interactive educational platform.
